ABOUT CYBER RESOURCES

Creating a platform to improve cybersecurity in Indonesia is a commendable goal! Building a centralized resource where individuals can find opportunities and companies can recruit professionals could greatly contribute to strengthening cybersecurity measures in the country.

Our company will use UU Cipta Kerja in accordance with Indonesia regulations to carry out recruitment, the aim to give individuals the flexibility to apply for job as a permanent or contract worker. We also provide a worker manager for HR department for easy monitoring company worker.
